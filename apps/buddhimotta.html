About this app

Buddhimotta is a Bengali language mobile application designed to assess Howard Gardner's eight multiple intelligences (MIs), offering a holistic approach to understanding individual strengths and weaknesses. The app employs a culturally relevant and accessible format, utilizing a 10-question Likert scale for each MI, covering both educational and general domains.

Users can evaluate their standing and areas for improvement by reviewing their overall scores in the eight MIs: linguistic, logical-mathematical, musical, spatial, bodily-kinesthetic, naturalistic, interpersonal, and intrapersonal intelligence. A pilot study with a diverse student sample revealed strong score distributions across various MIs, highlighting the app’s effectiveness in capturing individual differences and ensuring internal consistency. The intuitive design, engaging user interface, and insightful results are further validated by positive user feedback.

These findings suggest Buddhimotta’s potential to enhance self-awareness, facilitate personalized learning, guide educational and career decisions, and promote diverse learning environments. Its ease of use and accessibility make it a widely applicable tool, positively influencing student learning outcomes and fostering a more inclusive educational landscape.

Benefits

The app helps users identify their strengths in different subjects, understand their preferred learning styles, and recognize areas needing improvement. By analyzing their strong intelligences, students can gain insights into potential career paths.

Buddhimotta’s multiple intelligence assessment consists of engaging question sets for each of the eight intelligence types. Students can respond in a fun and interactive manner, allowing them to clearly determine their proficiency in each intelligence area.

Development Process

Research & Theory

Extensive research on Howard Gardner's Multiple Intelligences theory and consultation with education experts to develop a culturally relevant assessment methodology for Bengali students.

Development

Built with React Native and Firebase for an engaging user interface. Developed a comprehensive assessment system with 10-question Likert scales for each of the eight multiple intelligences.

Testing & Validation

Conducted a pilot study with diverse student groups to validate the assessment methodology. Analyzed score distributions and internal consistency to ensure the app effectively captures individual variations in multiple intelligences.
