About this app

Buddhimotta is an engaging brain training app that combines traditional Chakma puzzles with modern cognitive exercises. The app is designed to improve memory, problem-solving skills, and logical thinking while preserving cultural elements of the Chakma community.

Features

  • Over 50 unique puzzle types inspired by Chakma culture
  • Progressive difficulty levels for all ages
  • Daily challenges with rewards and achievements
  • Brain training exercises for memory, attention, and logic
  • Leaderboards and social sharing capabilities
  • Colorful, engaging visuals with cultural motifs

Development Process

Research & Concept

Researched traditional Chakma puzzles and games, consulting with community elders to identify authentic elements that could be adapted for a modern app.

Development

Built with Flutter and Dart for smooth animations and responsive design. Implemented a custom game engine to support various puzzle types and difficulty progression.

Testing & Optimization

Conducted extensive user testing across different age groups. Optimized performance for various devices and implemented analytics to track user engagement and puzzle difficulty.
